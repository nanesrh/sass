Black Hole

A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing, including light and other electromagnetic waves, has enough energy to escape it.

Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. It orbits at an average distance of 384,400 km, about 30 times Earth's diameter.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet and the furthest terrestrial planet from the Sun. The reddish color of its surface is due to finely grained iron(III) oxide dust in the soil.

Milky Way

The Milky Way is the galaxy that includes the Solar System with the name describing the galaxy's appearance from Earth.
